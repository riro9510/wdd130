Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide information and booking services for our rafting tours.

Audience

Our target audience includes adventure enthusiasts, thrill-seekers, families, and corporate groups looking for outdoor experiences. Three scenario questions to identify our target audience are:

  1. Are you interested in experiencing thrilling outdoor adventures?

    2. Answer: Yes, our target audience seeks adrenaline-pumping activities such as white water rafting.

  2. Are you planning a family vacation or group outing?

    3. Answer: Yes, families and groups often seek activities that offer excitement and bonding experiences.

  3. Are you looking for team-building activities for your company?

    4. Answer: Yes, corporate groups frequently seek outdoor team-building activities to foster teamwork and camaraderie.
